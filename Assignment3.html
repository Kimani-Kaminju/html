Famous African Cities

Nairobi,Kenya

Nairobi is the capital city of Kenya and one of the fastest-growing cities in Africa.It is known as the "Green City in the sun" because of its beautiful environment and parks.Nairobi is a major center for business,technology and tourism

  • Country:Kenya
  • Nickname:Green city in the Sun
  • Population:Over 4 Million people
  • Main Activities:Business and Technology
  • Attraction Sites: Nairobi National Park,Nairobi Museum and Snake Park
  • Main Languages:English,Swahili

Cairo,Egypt

Cairo is the capital city of Egypt and one of the largets cities in Africa.It is famous for The Pyramids,River Nile and Ancient History

  1. Country:Egypt
  2. Population:Over 10 Million people
  3. Main Activities:Tourism
  4. Attraction Sites:The Pyramids of Giza
  5. Main Languages:Arabic,English

Kigali,Rwanda

Kigali is the capital city of Rwanda.It is known for being the most cleanest and most organised city in Africa

  1. Country:Rwanda
  2. Population:Over 1 Million
  3. Main Activities:Business and Tourism
  4. Best Known for:Clean and organised environment
  5. Main Languages:Kinyarwanda,English

Lagos,Nigeria

Lagos is a major city in Nigeria known for Business,Entertainment and busy Urban Life

  • Country:Nigeria
  • Population:Over 15 Million people
  • Main Activities:Trade and Entertainment
  • Best Known for:Music and Business
  • Main Languages:English,Yoruba
