Nganya Culture In Kenya

Nganya culture is popular in Nairobi and other towns in Kenya. Nganyas are colorful matatus decorated with graffiti, bright lights, loud music and pictures of celebrities or musicians.

Many young people enjoy riding Nganyas because they provide entertainment during travel. Some matatus also have televison screens, powerful sound systems and unique interior designs

Transport Information

Road Transport
Road transport is the most common form of transport in Kenya. People use cars,buses,matatus and motorcycles daily
Railway Transport
Kenya uses railway transport to move passengers and goods.The Standard Gauge Railway (SGR) has improved travel between Nairobi and Mombasa
Air Transport
Air Transport is fast and mainly used for long distance travel and tourism.Major airports include Jomo Kenyatta International Airport and Moi International Airport
Challenges Facing Transport
Some challenges facing transport incluide traffic jam,road accidents,poor roads in some areas and high fuel prices
